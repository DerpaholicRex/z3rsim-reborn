About

Original Simulator

Z3R Simulator was originally created by Kyong92 as a fast way to practice A Link to the Past Randomizer routing. The idea was simple: keep the feel of a real seed, but make each run short enough that you could practice decisions in minutes instead of hours.

The original creator described the project as a way to focus on the routing layer itself. Playing full seeds is fun, but it can be hard to repeat spots, compare choices, or catch mistakes while also piloting the game. A simulator makes those decisions easier to see.

The design was heavily inspired by Crossproduct's Randomizer Tracker. The game logic is currently based on the VT v31 version of the Randomizer.

Z3RSim is meant to be mobile friendly, so you can play during a commute or squeeze in a quick seed before sleeping.

Hopefully you have as much fun with Rando as the community has had with it.

Reborn

The original site went quiet near the end of 2023. Z3RS Reborn is focused on continuing the work, keeping the tool up to date and useful.

What exists now is the result of community recovery work: preserving the site, deobfuscating the code, updating the app, and starting to make it maintainable again. It still has work to do, but the goal is the same: a fast, approachable way to think through Randomizer routing.

If you have any suggestions, questions, bug reports or just want to say hi, feel free to join the Z3R Community Discord.
